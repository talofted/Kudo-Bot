What is Kudo?

Kudo is a fun card-based bot that allows users to collect, trade, and play with unique cards featuring popular anime and game characters!

Available on Discord and soon on Twitch, Kudo offers a variety of features, including special edition holographic cards, custom card packs, and an interactive experience with both players and CPU opponents.
